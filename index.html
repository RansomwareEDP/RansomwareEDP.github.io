Research Classification
Built to identify where the ecosystem breaks — and how to push it there.

The Ransomware Ecosystem Dependency Project maps the structural dependencies that sustain Russia/CIS-linked ransomware operations — financial flows, infrastructure layers, jurisdictional protection, and the criminal service economy that underpins them.

The goal is not just to understand the ecosystem. It is to identify leverage points where targeted pressure produces measurable degradation.
